Air-Conditioner Issues & Thier Possible Reasons

High Temperature

  • Faulty compressor
  • Dirty coils

Voltage/Frequency Fluctuation

  • Grid instability
  • Faulty wiring

High Humidity

  • Clogged drain
  • Low airflow

Excess Dust

  • Dirty filters
  • Poor ventilation

Abnormal Blower RPM

  • Motor issue
  • Capacitor failure

Excess Vibration

  • Loose parts
  • Unbalanced blower

Coil Freezing

  • Low refrigerant
  • Airflow blockage
